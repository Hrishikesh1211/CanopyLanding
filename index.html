What it is

Reading deserves a better home.

Goodreads was built in 2007 and hasn't meaningfully changed since. It's cluttered, socially awkward, and designed like a database — not a reading life.

Canopy is built on a different belief: that your reading history is one of the most personal things you own, and it deserves to be kept with genuine care.

No followers. No recommendation engine. No performance. Just your books, on a shelf that grows more beautiful — and more yours — the longer you use it.

A shelf that feels real

Every book cover rendered with care. A visual, scrollable shelf — not a list, not a spreadsheet.

One thought per book

Not a review. Not a rating prompt. Just one line — the thought that stayed with you. Nothing more is asked.

Your year in books

A beautiful annual summary — like Spotify Wrapped, but for the books that actually shaped your year.
